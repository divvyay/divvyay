about me...



Hello! My name is Divya Krishnan, a cartographer, graphic designer, and geography nerd.

I am a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Data Science, Cartography, Geographical Information Systems, and Geography. I am also studying towards minors in French and Environmental Studies.

I am very interested in both technology and design, and most importantly the intersection of the two. I have built up experience with both skillsets. I am currently a cartographer for UW-Madison and a graphic designer for Wisconsin Engineer.

My other hobbies and interests include traveling, geopolitical news, video games, going on bike rides, calligraphy, and playing the piano.
